Ducks

Ducks are aquatic birds that are found all over the world. They are known for their distinctive bills, webbed feet, and waterproof feathers.

Types of Ducks

  • Mallard
  • Pekin
  • Rouen
  • Muscovy

Duck Behavior

Ducks are social animals that often gather in flocks. They are also known for their courtship displays, which can involve head bobbing, tail wagging, and vocalizations.

Duck Fun Facts

  • The fastest duck is the canvasback, which can reach speeds of up to 70 km/h (43 mph).
  • Ducks have a specialized gland, called the preen gland, that produces oil that they use to waterproof their feathers.
  • Most ducks have highly sensitive bills that contain nerve endings, allowing them to detect prey underwater.
